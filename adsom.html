A DARKER SHADE OF MAGIC

BOOK DESIGN

A redesign of A Darker Shade of Magic by V.E. Schwab.

A Darker Shade of Magic is an adventure of a magician and a thief in four parallel Londons: white, red, gray, and black. This book cover redesign captures the different Londons and each stacked on top of one another.

FOUR LONDONS

WHITE LONDON

A starving world where magic is abused and color and joy drained.

The background represents White London.

RED LONDON

A copious world flourising with magic and prospers with the help of magic.

"MAGIC" represents Red London.

GRAY LONDON

A mundane world where magic is forgotten, and magic is scarce.

"SHADE" represents Gray London.

BLACK LONDON

A dead world crushed out of life and consumed by its greed for magic.

The word "DARKER" represents Black London.
